About Nizwa

Nizwa, known as the social capital of Oman, is an interesting city that offers a brief look into the rich legacy of the country. The impressive Nizwa Fort, one of its historic landmarks, bears testimony to its long history. The clamoring Nizwa Souq, renowned for its energetic air and customary handiworks, is a must-visit for vacationers looking for a genuine Omani encounter.
